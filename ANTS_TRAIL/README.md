# Trail formation in ants by pheromone deposition

https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/76469594/197138410-42dc0e20-df43-46f3-a4fb-6e1c7d6000ff.mp4


Studying trail formation in ants by pheromone deposition and detection. Model of ants moving on a 2D periodic and continuous plane,
leaving and detecting pheromones, and stochastically adjusting their direction based on pheromone
concentration. Trails emerge under certain parameter ranges.
